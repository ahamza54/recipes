Ganache Cake

Ingredients

  • Eggs (2)
  • All Purpose Flour(1cup)
  • Cocao Powder(3/4cup)
  • Sugar(2cups)
  • Salt(1tbsp)
  • Vegitable Oil(1/2cup)
  • Baking Powder(2tbsp)

Steps

  1. Mix Ingredients in the provided quantities.
  2. Use spatula to check for lumps.
  3. Pour batter in cake tin.
  4. Bake till crust is hard and not sticky to the touch.
