Bakes Chicken Pasta

Ingredients

  • Penne Pasta
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • All Purpose Flour
  • Garlic
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Boil Pasta in a deep Pan
  2. Fry Finely-Diced garlic in Oil
  3. Add Flour once Golden
  4. Add Milk and Cheese to create enough white sauce
  5. Add Boiled Pasta
  6. Add Black Pepper
  7. Add Seasoning
  8. Sprinkle Cheese and bake till the Cheese is golden on top
